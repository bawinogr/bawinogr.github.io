Research

I am a current graduate student in theoretical chemistry at the University of Michigan. My primary research area is electronic structure theory.

Chem Ed Research

I am also interested in Chemistry Education research. Students who work interactively with the concepts they are learning have an opportunity to understand those topics more deeply. I develop teaching demonstrations using Mathematica and help train students to make their own demos. A few examples of demostrations I have developed are here:

  • Isobaric Compression And Expansion Of An Ideal Gas
  • Work Done In Reversible And Irreversible Compression Of An Ideal Gas
  • Adiabatic Expansion And Compression Of An Ideal Gas
  • Reversible And Irreversible Isothermal Expansion Of An Ideal Gas
